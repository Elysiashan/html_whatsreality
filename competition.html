Competition

Big Brother

From CBS

About: A group of contestants move into the Big Brother house and nominate 2-3 people each week to be evicted. These contestants are isolated from the outside world and are under constant surveillance by cameras. They must compete in 2 challenges each week to gain safety for the week and gain power in the game. One of the challenges is the Head of Household competition and the winner of that game gets full power of the week. They can nominate whoever they want for the block. The second challenge is called the Power of Veto and if a contestant that’s on the block wins this competition, they get the power to take themselves off the running for eviction. If a contestant wins the competition that is not up for eviction, they have the power to take someone else off or leave the nominations the same. The HOH can then nominate someone else for eviction if someone takes themselves off. The contestants that aren’t nominated can vote for who they want to leave and then the contestant who receives the majority votes leave the house.

Show host: Julie Chen Moonves | Best seasons:BB10, BB14, BB16

The Amazing Race

From CBS

About: A group of contestants are paired in groups of two, typically the pairs are family members or loved ones. These pairs will race around the world completing competitions to advance to the next race or “leg”. After each leg the last pair to make it to the last pit stop is eliminated from the game and goes home. In each leg there are typically a few challenges either physical or mental and places they must make it to, sometimes they must ask strangers to translate and show them directions. The last team standings win a cash grand prize.

Show host: Phil Koeghan | Best seasons:12, 10, 20

Survivor

From CBS

About: A group of contestants are stranded on a deserted island trying to live while also competing in a challenge to earn immunity from being voted off. It begins with 2 teams, and the teams compete in competitions, and the losing team goes to tribal council where they have to vote off one of their teammates. They also have the chance to find a hidden immunity idol that gives a contestant safety at tribal council and is usually hidden near their campsite. In addition to the competitions they have to know basic life skills to survive. For example, how to build shelter, find food, and survive outside while having allies and a social game.

Show host: Jeff Probst | Best seasons: 17, 7, 15
